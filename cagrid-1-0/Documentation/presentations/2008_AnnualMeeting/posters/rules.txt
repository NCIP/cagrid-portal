Poster Session Abstract

Please review the information below and then complete this form to submit a poster abstract for consideration.

    * The posters will be displayed for the duration of the conference with set up by 5:00pm on Monday, June 23 and tear down after 1:00pm on Wednesday, June 25.
    * Each successful applicant will be given a designated time slot during which she/he will be required to present her/his poster and respond to questions from participants.
    * Each poster must be displayed within a 4 foot high x 4 foot wide space.
    * The abstracts will be reviewed in April and selections will be announced by May 1.
    * If selected, all information provided will be printed in the meeting materials exactly as you enter it.
    * Additional information about exact dates and times, shipping instructions, etc. will be provided to proposers of accepted abstracts.
