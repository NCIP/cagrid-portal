                  1.0 caGrid Portal Introduction
================================================================

    This document contains the release notes for the caGrid 
    Portal 2.0.2.1 release. This is a minor bug fix release of
    caGrid Portal 2.0.2

    This release fixes some bugs and known issues
    with the previous release (2.0.2).



================================================================
                      2.0 Release History
================================================================

    caGrid Portal 1.1 was released with caGrid 1.1 on 17th December 2007
    caGrid Portal 2.0 was release on December 28 2007
    caGrid Portal 2.0.1 was released on Feb 22 2008
    caGrid Portal 2.0.2 was released on June 18 2008

                     5.0 Known Issues Not Yet Fixed
================================================================

Update to date bug reports and feature requests can be found on the tracker section
of the project's gforge site:
http://gforge.nci.nih.gov/tracker/?group_id=25

 * Some Porlets do not mantain strict screen width and can get too wide
 and distort other portlets on the same page
 * Service unbanning operation will sometimes fail for services that have
 been banned for a long time (greater than 4 days).

                	6.0 Bug Reports and Support
================================================================

	The preferred approach is to first search the user's email
	archives for your problem, then email your question to the
	list.  Bugs can also be entered on the gforge project page.
	
	User's listserv archive:
	https://list.nih.gov/archives/cagrid_users-l.html
	
	Join listserv:
	https://list.nih.gov/cgi-bin/wa?SUBED1=cagrid_users-l&A=1
