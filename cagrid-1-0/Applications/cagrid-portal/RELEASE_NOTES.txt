================================================================
		1.0 caGrid Portal Introduction
================================================================

   This document contains the release notes for the caGrid
   Portal 2.2 release.

   This release of caGrid Portal consolidates and extends the
   feature sets of earlier release of the caGrid Portal.

================================================================
		2.0 Release History
================================================================

    caGrid Portal 1.1   was released on 17 December 2007
    caGrid Portal 2.0   was released on 28 December 2008
    caGrid Portal 2.0.1 was released on 22 February 2008
    caGrid Portal 2.0.2 was released on 18 June 2008
    caGrid Portal 2.1   was released on 2nd October 2008

================================================================
		3.0 Enhancements Since Last Release
================================================================

   Specific highlighted improvements of caGrid Portal 2.2 include:

   * Improved performance: enabled aggressive caching of
     information and tuned database.
   * Upgraded Liferay Portal to 5.0.1
   * Add a "Sign in" and "Register" link to all pages.
   * Implemented Section 508 Compliance recommendations.

================================================================
		6.0 Bug Reports and Support
================================================================

   The preferred approach is to first search the user's email
   archives for your problem before posting your question to
   the Knowledge Center forums. Bugs can also be entered on
   the gforge project page.

   caGrid Knowledge Center Forums:
      https://cabig-kc.nci.nih.gov/CaGrid/forums/

   gforge Project Page:
      http://gforge.nci.nih.gov/projects/cagrid-1-0/

   Submit an Enhancement Request or Report a Bug:
      http://gforge.nci.nih.gov/tracker/?group_id=25
