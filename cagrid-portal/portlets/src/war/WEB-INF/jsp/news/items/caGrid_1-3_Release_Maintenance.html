Production Grid Maintenance 3/25 to 3/29

In preparation for the release of caGrid 1.3, NIHnet engineers will perform maintenance on the NCI caBIG(r) Production Grid beginning at 6:00 a.m on Wednesday March 25, 2009 and continuing through Sunday March 29, 2009. During this period, the NCI caBIG(r) Production Grid may be unavailable.

When: 6:00 a.m. Eastern on Wednesday March 25, 2009 through 6:00 p.m. Eastern on Sunday March 29, 2009

Areas Affected: NCI Production Grid, and any dependent applications.

Reason: Preparation for the release of caGrid 1.3

During this period, developers may choose to target the Training Grid, hosted at Ohio State University.
